Yongjian Fu「付永健」

I am an incoming postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, working with Prof. Ju Ren. I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the CSE department at the Central South University, advised by Prof. Ju Ren. Prior to that, I received my bachelor degree from Central South University in 2021. I worked closely with Prof. Lili Qiu at MSRA from 2023 to 2024, and with Prof. Xinyu Zhang at UCSD as a visiting scholar from 2024 to 2025.

Email   /   Google Scholar   /   Resume   /   Github     

Research Topics

My research interests lie in AIoT, Edge Intelligence, and Embodied & Agentic AI. I am particularly interested in designing efficient systems that bridge the gap between the physical world and AI agents through the tight integration of sensing, inference, and interaction across devices and environments.
